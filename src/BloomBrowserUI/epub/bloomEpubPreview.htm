Bloom EPUB Output is an experimental feature. We have included it in this version of Bloom in the hopes that you will try it out and give us feedback that will guide future development.

Are you interetested in in distributing Bloom books for use on phones and other ebook readers? Is there something you would need us to do before it is useful for you? Please use the Help:Report A Problem command to send us feedback. Thanks!

Notes:

  • You can resize the phone to see how pages might appear.
  • On phones, we recommend you try the Gitden Reader. Dropbox and similar services are a handy way to move bloom epubs to your phone. Just click Save EPUB and then choose your Dropbox folder. On your phone, open the Dropbox app and select your book.
  • This page uses the Readium epub reader. Books will likely look different on different readers.
  • This tool will automatically make a talking book if you use the Talking Book tool to record your book.
