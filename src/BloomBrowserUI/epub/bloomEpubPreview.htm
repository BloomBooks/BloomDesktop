Getting this book onto your Device

A handy way to get books to your phone is to set up Dropbox or similar service on both your computer and your phone/tablet. Just click Save EPUB and then choose your Dropbox folder. On your phone, open the Dropbox app and select your book. If you are using a laptop, you can also try the free ShareIt program to send files from your laptop to a phone or tablet.

Recommended Reader

Our testing has shown Gitden Reader to be a good choice for Android and IOS (IPhone & IPad) devices.

Notes

  • This page uses the Readium epub reader. Books will likely look different on different readers.
  • This tool will automatically make a "Talking Book" if you use the Talking Book tool to add voice recordings.
  • The preview here cannot yet play Talking Books. You can listen to it using Adobe's Free Digital Editions e-book reader.
  • To listen to a Talking Book using Gitden on a phone or table, you will need to disable Gitden's "Text To Speech" feature before it will show you the audio controls.
