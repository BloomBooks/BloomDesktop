La casquette est restée prise sur la branche d’un énorme vieil arbre.

And I did not eat my dinner.

 
 

J’ai beaucoup pleuré. J’ai même refusé de manger pour le souper.

I had to smile too.

"The moon sent it," she said.

That night, both the moon and
I wore our caps and smiled.
We were happy.
